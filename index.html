We’re here for you: 9 ways we can help you recover from this pandemic

We realize the event industry has never faced challenges like this before. We are committed to doing everything we can to help you weather this setback without spending a dime.

1. Generate Cash Quickly At No Cost

Do you have a product/service that could help give a boost to healthcare heroes? We created a platform called HonorAndThank.com where anyone can post a message (text, video or image) to a healthcare hero. Once someone has submitted a message, they will see a pop-up that lists local vendors who could send a meal, flowers, balloons, desserts etc. You would just need to complete the “Sell healthcare hero” field when you complete your category profile and indicate how someone can place an order with you. There is no charge and we’re not involved in the transaction. You can use this press release to promote this in your area.

2. Get insight, strategies, resources & hope from fellow vendors.

Join our Facebook Group for vendors – Wedding & Event Vendors: Stronger Together. We have a team researching and creating helpful resources for you.

3. Introduce Yourself

Videos are now available to vendors with free profiles. This is a great way for you to introduce yourself when meeting in person may not be possible. You can upload one right from your phone. Videos is now critical in marketing.

4. Connect Instantly With Prospects

Our instant chat feature is now free for all vendors. Once users feel comfortable setting a date for a gathering, they’re going to want to aggressively book vendors.
